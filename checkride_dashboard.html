Zapier setup instructions

1. In Kajabi, go to Settings, then Integrations, then Webhooks. Create a new webhook for New Purchase events pointed at your Zapier webhook URL.

2. In Zapier, create a Zap with Trigger set to Kajabi New Purchase and Action set to Google Sheets Add Row. Map three fields: product name, amount, and date.

3. In Google Sheets, go to File, then Share, then Publish to web. Select your sheet, choose CSV format, and copy the URL into the field above.

Once wired, revenue entries will sync automatically every time this dashboard loads.
