Zapier setup

1. In Kajabi go to Settings, Integrations, Webhooks. Create a New Purchase webhook pointed at your Zapier webhook URL.

2. In Zapier: Trigger is Kajabi New Purchase, Action is Google Sheets Create Row. Map product name, amount, and date.

3. In Google Sheets go to File, Share, Publish to web. Choose CSV format and paste the URL above.
